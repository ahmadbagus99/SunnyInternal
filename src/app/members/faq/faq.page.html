What is Sunny CRM?

Strategy for managing organizational relationships and interactions with customers and potential customers. The SUNNY CRM system is Speeding up the processing of CRM and getting information about the number of prospects existing, then get a 360 degree view of the Customer. And make it easier for Customers / Prospect segmentation.

What are Sunny CRM Functional Systems?

1. The System can manage Customer data (Leads).

2. The System can manage Customer user (Contact, Company, Supplier, and partner).

3. The System can manage Prospect.

4. The System can manage Activity (New leads and old leads, meeting schedules, times and places)

5. The system used is very User Friendly
